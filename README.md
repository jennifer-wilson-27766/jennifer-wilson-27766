<!-- ========== COURSES ========== -->
## 📖 My Courses — AY 2025–2026

> 🎯 _3 courses per semester • 9 credit hours • Focus: Databases, Accounting IS & Web_

<div align="center">

### 🍂 Fall 2025 — Semester 1

</div>

> **① 120022 — Hệ thống thông tin quản lý** *(Management Information Systems)* `3 credits`
>
> Concepts of information systems in organizations, system components, IS strategy, decision support systems, and e-business models.
>
> **② 120024 — Kế toán máy** *(Computerized Accounting)* `4 credits`
>
> Hands-on practice with accounting software (MISA, FAST Accounting, SAP). Covers journal entries, ledger management, and financial report generation.
>
> **③ 120034 — Hệ thống thông tin kế toán** *(Accounting Information Systems)* `3 credits`
>
> Design and evaluation of accounting information systems, internal controls, transaction cycles, and AIS security.

---

<div align="center">

### 🌸 Spring 2026 — Semester 2

</div>

> **④ 120029 — Hệ quản trị cơ sở dữ liệu** *(Database Management Systems)* `3 credits`
>
> SQL Server administration, indexing, stored procedures, triggers, backup & recovery, performance tuning.
>
> **⑤ 120025 — Lập trình kế toán** *(Accounting Programming)* `4 credits`
>
> Developing accounting applications using C# and .NET, connecting to databases, generating automated reports.
>
> **⑥ 120051 — Thương mại điện tử** *(E-Commerce)* `2 credits`
>
> E-commerce business models, payment gateways, online security, digital marketing integration, and platform development.
